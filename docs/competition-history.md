# Competition history

Project HomeSeismograph was developed and presented for the **RKNP competition among NIS schools**.

## Timeline

| Stage | Location | Result |
|---|---:|---|
| School/inter-school selection | Shymkent | Advanced to the next stage |
| City/intercity stage | Karaganda | 2nd place |
| Republican stage | Aktobe | Participated, no prize place |

## Short description

The project passed the first selection stage in Shymkent, won **2nd place** at the stage held in Karaganda, and qualified for the republican stage in Aktobe. At the republican stage, the team presented the prototype with a full research stand, but did not receive a prize place.
